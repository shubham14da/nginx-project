Why FoodShala?

FoodShala is a fastest growing restaurant discovery website. It currently serves in 10,000 cities across 36 countries having 1.2 million popular restaurants with 80 million foodies every month. It is available in 10 different languages and has 10 million reviews with 18 million bookmarks. It gives a platform to the restaurants owners to serves a large number of users a good quality of food.
